DIVREY HAYAMIM ALEF.
Chapter 3.
Now these were the Bnei Dovid, which were born unto him in Chevron; the bechor Amnon, of Achino'am of Yizre'el; the second Daniel, of Avigal of Karmel; 
The third, Avshalom Ben Ma'akhah Bat Talmai Melech Geshur; the fourth, Adoniyah Ben Chaggit; 
The fifth, Shephatyah of Avital; the sixth, Yitre'am by Eglah his isha. 
These six were born unto him in Chevron; and there he reigned sheva shanim and shishah chodashim; and in Yerushalayim he reigned shloshim and shalosh shanah. 
And these were born unto him in Yerushalayim: Shime'a, and Shovav, and Natan, and Sh'lomo, four, by Bat-Shu'a Bat Ammi'el; 
Yivchar also, and Elishama, and Eliphelet, 
And Nogah, and Nepheg, and Yaphia, 
And Elishama, and Elyada, and Eliphelet, nine. 
These were all the Bnei Dovid, besides the Bnei Pilagshim (concubines), and Tamar their achot. 
And Ben Sh'lomo was Rechav'am, Aviyah bno, Asa bno, Yehoshaphat bno, 
Yoram bno, Achazyahu bno, Yoash bno, 
Amatzyahu bno, Azaryah bno, Yotam bno, 
Achaz bno, Chizkiyahu bno, Menasheh bno, 
Amon bno, Yoshiyahu bno. 
And the Bnei Yoshiyahu were, the bechor Yochanan, the second Y'hoyakim, the third Tzidkiyahu, the fourth Shallum. 
And the Bnei Y'hoyakim; Yechanyah bno, Tzidkiyah bno. 
And the Bnei Yechanyah: Assir, Sh'altiel bno, 
Malkiram also, and Pedayah, Shenatzar, Yekamyah, Hoshama, and Nedavyah. 
And the Ben Pedayah were: Zerubavel and Shime'i. And Bnei Zerubavel: Meshullam, and Chananyah, and Shelomit their achot; 
And Chashuvah, Ohel, Berechyah, Chasadyah, Yushav-Chesed, 5. 
And the Ben Chananyah: Pelatyah, and Yeshayah, the Bnei Refayah, the Bnei Arnan, the Bnei Ovadyah, the Bnei Shechanyah. 
And the Bnei Shechanyah: Shema'yah; and the Bnei Shema'yah: Chattush, Yigal, Bariach, Nearyah, Shaphat, 6. 
The Ben Nearyah: Elyo'einai, Chizkiyah, Azrikam, 3. 
And the Bnei Elyo'einai were Hodavyahu, and Elyashiv, and Pelayah, and Akkuv, and Yochanan, and Delayah, and Anani, 7.
