Lord, you enkindled in the heart of Blessed Marie Rose Durocher the
flame of ardent charity and a great desire to collaborate, as teacher,
in the mission of the Church. Grant us that same active love, so that,
in responding to the needs of the world today, we may lead our brothers
and sisters to eternal life. We ask this through our Lord Jesus Christ,
your Son, who lives and reigns with you and the Holy Spirit, God, for
ever and ever.
