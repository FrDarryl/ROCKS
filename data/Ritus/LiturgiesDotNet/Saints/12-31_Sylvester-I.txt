Come, O Lord, to the help of your people, sustained by the intercession
of Pope Saint Sylvester, so that, running the course of this present
life under your guidance we may happily attain life without end.
Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son, who lives and reigns with you
in the unity of the Holy Spirit, God, for ever and ever.
